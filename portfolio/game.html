Game Ads

Animations created for mobile game advertisement

Working at Kolibri Games and freelancing for Voodoo, I created many, many, mobile game ads. These animations are approximately 30 seconds long, highly engaging, and designed with one goal in mind, to get viewers to click. Mobile gaming ads are highly performance driven and often the most outlandish ideas are the ones that capture viewers attention.
